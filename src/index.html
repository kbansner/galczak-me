The Hygienist's Sabbatical Service

Want to travel or take a sabbatical but don't want to lose your job? We offer a unique service for dental hygienists: we'll house-sit, pet-sit, and provide temporary job coverage so you can take a trip without worry.

Imagine taking that long-dreamed-of trip to Europe, Asia, or South America—or simply taking time off to care for a family member. Instead of quitting your job or rushing through a vacation, we provide experienced, professional care for your home and your practice. We'll step in and cover your hygiene schedule while you're away, ensuring your patients receive continuous, high-quality care.

Why This Helps Your Practice

  • Employee Retention: Help your best hygienists take a break without losing them. This service is a valuable benefit that can increase loyalty and prevent burnout.
  • Expert Coverage: Get a seasoned professional with over 20 years of experience to provide seamless, expert coverage.
  • Fresh Perspective: Benefit from new ideas and best practices brought by a professional who has worked in dozens of dental offices across the West Coast.

Why This Helps the Hygienist

  • Travel and Sabbatical: Don't wait until retirement to take that extended trip. Two weeks is never enough time for a true adventure, but our service allows you to be away for 1 to 5 months.
  • Peace of Mind: Take a leave for any reason—whether it's for travel or to care for an elder family member—without the stress of finding job coverage or worrying about your home, pets, and plants. Your job and house will be taken care of while you're away.
